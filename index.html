About Nordico Nights

We're super excited to be launching Nordico Nights tomorrow evening. We'll be hearing testimonies about how Jesus has impacted people's lives, how God has wisdom for now and what the good news of Jesus is all about.
It's 7pm at the Nordico Lounge in Ormskirk, we'll be providing free cake and a great welcome. It'll be a great event to bring friends to hear about Jesus for the first time or the 101st time.
